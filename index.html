Welcome to our Backyard

What started as a weekend hobby with two vines, has become eight acres of blooming grapes, walking trails, a restaurant and event space, and our happy way of life. Felix planted our first vines 15 years ago, and we were hooked after producing our first case of wine.

After our first vineyard expansion in 2015, we opened our tasting room and restaurant so guests could enjoy farm-to-table snacks while enjoying our wines and the spectacular views. The views begged to be explored, so we set to work incorporating 6 miles of walking trails, in collaboration with Rocky Hills Nature Center to ensure Mother Nature stayed protected.

We welcome you to experience Backyard Winery as you please; try our wines, explore, relax, eat and make yourself at home.
-- Elena & Felix

Our Wines

Eight Acres of Sauvignon Blanc, Merlot and Cabernet Sauvignon

We're fortunate to yield enough high-quality fruit to estate bottle all our wines. Our priorities are great grapes, grown from healthy soil. Our team practices a careful vetting of the growing practices in the vineyard, to minimal-impact extraction, aging, and storage.

Discover your next favorite wine at our tasting room, an inviting setting designed to give you a personalized tasting experience.

Visit

Our tasting room, restaurant and grounds are open year-round

Hang out in our tasting room or adjacent deck and savor the exceptional Backyard Wines pulled from our winery.
